Privacy Policy 隱私權政策

Effective date: August 1, 2026 ｜ 生效日：2026 年 8 月 1 日

1. What we collect ｜ 我們蒐集什麼

Account details (name, email, grade, school type), the learning-portfolio content you choose to capture (notes, files, reflections, college lists), AI coaching conversations, and basic service logs (timestamps, feature usage, token counts for billing our AI provider). We do not collect data from your device beyond what you submit, and we do not track you across other sites.

帳號資料（姓名、Email、年級、學校類型）、你選擇記錄的學習歷程內容（筆記、檔案、反思、選校清單）、AI 教練對話，以及基本服務紀錄（時間戳、功能使用、供 AI 服務計費的 token 數）。除了你主動提交的內容，我們不從你的裝置蒐集其他資料，也不做跨站追蹤。

2. How we use it ｜ 我們如何使用

Only to operate and improve the Service: storing your portfolio, generating AI coaching feedback, showing teachers you work with your submissions for certification, sending service notifications (e.g., LINE or email reminders you opt into), and processing subscriptions. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use student content for advertising.

僅用於營運與改善本服務：儲存你的歷程、產生 AI 教練回饋、讓與你合作的老師審閱你送出的認證素材、寄送你同意接收的服務通知（如 LINE 或 Email 提醒），以及處理訂閱。我們不出售個人資料，也不將學生內容用於廣告。

3. Who can see your data ｜ 誰看得到你的資料

You, and the TIPS staff/teachers supporting you. Parents see progress only through summaries the student generates. We never share your portfolio externally.

你本人，以及協助你的 TIPS 老師與人員。家長僅透過學生自行產出的進度摘要了解進度。我們絕不對外提供你的歷程內容。

4. Service providers ｜ 委託處理者

We rely on: Google (Workspace/Apps Script — data storage and hosting), Anthropic (AI coaching — your prompts and relevant portfolio context are sent to generate feedback; not used to train their models per our API terms), Lemon Squeezy (payments — we never see your card number), LINE (optional notifications), and GitHub Pages (website hosting). Each receives only what its function requires.

我們使用：Google（Workspace／Apps Script——資料儲存與主機）、Anthropic（AI 教練——為產生回饋會傳送你的提問與相關歷程內容；依 API 條款不用於訓練模型）、Lemon Squeezy（金流——我們不會看到你的卡號）、LINE（選用的通知）、GitHub Pages（網站託管）。各服務僅取得其功能所需的資料。

5. Minors ｜ 未成年人

The Service is designed for students in grades 8–12. Users under 13 require verifiable parental consent, and parents may review or request deletion of their child's data at any time.

本服務為 8–12 年級學生設計。未滿 13 歲須取得家長可驗證之同意；家長可隨時檢視或要求刪除孩子的資料。

6. Retention, access, deletion ｜ 保存、查閱與刪除

Your portfolio stays as long as your account exists — including without an active subscription. You may request a copy of your data, or full account deletion, by contacting us; we delete within 30 days except records we must keep by law (e.g., billing records).

只要帳號存在，你的歷程就會保存——即使沒有有效訂閱。你可以聯絡我們索取資料副本或要求刪除帳號；除依法必須保存的紀錄（如帳務）外，我們會在 30 天內完成刪除。

7. Security ｜ 資訊安全

Data is stored on Google infrastructure with access limited to authorized TIPS staff. Passwords are never displayed back, and API keys are kept in server-side configuration. No system is perfectly secure — if a breach affecting your data occurs, we will notify you promptly.

資料儲存於 Google 基礎設施，僅授權的 TIPS 人員可存取。密碼不會回顯，API 金鑰僅存於伺服器端設定。沒有系統能保證絕對安全——若發生影響你資料的安全事件，我們會儘速通知你。

8. Changes and contact ｜ 政策變更與聯絡

Material changes will be announced in-app or by email at least 14 days before taking effect. Questions or data requests: contact us via tips-edu.com or LINE.

重大變更將於生效前至少 14 天在站內或以 Email 公告。任何隱私問題或資料請求，請透過 tips-edu.com 或 LINE 與我們聯絡。
